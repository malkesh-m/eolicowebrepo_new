ARTBIDER

Artbider is one of the fastest emerging online service of the auction market for fine art, antiques and collectibles. Artbider offers access to more than 10 millions auctioned records, dating back to 1970. Our team of fine art specialists, art historians and worlds best art data analyst working hard to compile the most up-to-date and precise Art Market information.

ArtBidder Offers

Artbider offers a keyword-search to our comprehensive price database of over 10 million auctioned records with classified details – Title, Categories, Medium, Artwork Dates etc. You can access more than 500,000 artists records, with images, bio’s and art genre. Artbider covers more than 1000 top auction houses globally.

We send daily alerts of your favourite artists on their upcoming artworks and events. You will not be missing any of your favourite Auctions and Events now.

Artbider keeps you ahead with the latest art market updates – Upcoming Auctions, News and Events.

Add and save your favourite artworks and artists to your collection. Artbider allows you to build and manage your own collection of your favourite Artworks, Artists and Auctions.

Get the insights of your favourite artist annual performance – total lots sold, average sold price, selling rate and more to make better and precise decisions.
